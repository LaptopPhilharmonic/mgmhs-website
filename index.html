I offer private, self-pay, mental health care with a flexible approach. I can meet you wherever you feel comfortable - in your own home or somewhere else.

How I Work

I work with all people one-to-one, in couples or with families. I offer individual sessions for people aged from 10 upwards, and family/parenting support or behavioural work prior to this age.

As a qualified nurse with over 12 years' experience in child and adolescent psychiatry within the NHS, I have a wealth of knowledge to draw on. I'll work with you to create a personal therapeutic plan to help you towards the goals we establish.

I can help with:

  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Neurodiversity
  • Trauma
  • Attachment difficulties
  • Psychosis
  • Emotional regulation
  • Developing social skills
  • Self-harm
  • Gender Identity Support
  • LGBTQ+
  • Transition to Secondary schools
  • Coping strategies
  • Other complex presentations

About me: Michelle Hunt RMN, BSc (Hons)

I earned my first class Nursing Degree at London Southbank University.

I have over 12 years experience within the NHS where I specialised in Child and Adolescent Psychiatry. In my time as a student nurse, I quickly realised how passionate I was about helping young people and their families.

My experience doesn't stop with young people though - I've worked with adults, both in the community and in hospitals, through both the NHS and private health services.

I've worked with all manner of people, including high risk, crisis and long-term cases.

My interests and expertise cover the following areas:

  • Child and Adolescent Mental Health
  • Young Adult Mental Health
  • Transition
  • Neurodiversity
  • Group Therapy

  • Psychosis
