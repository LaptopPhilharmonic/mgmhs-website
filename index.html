About me: Michelle Hunt RMN, BSc (Hons)

I earned my first class Nursing Degree at London Southbank University.

I have over 12 years experience within the NHS where I specialised in Child and Adolescent Psychiatry. In my time as a student nurse, I quickly realised how passionate I was about helping young people and their families.

My experience doesn't stop with young people though - I've worked with adults, both in the community and in hospitals, through both the NHS and private health services.

I've worked with all manner of people, including high risk, crisis and long-term cases.

My interests and expertise cover the following areas:

  • Child and Adolescent Mental Health
  • Young Adult Mental Health
  • Transition
  • Neurodiversity
  • Group Therapy
